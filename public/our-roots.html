Our Roots

Why “HayTree”?

Our name is a blend of tech, love, and family. When we first started living together, our cozy second-floor apartment was surrounded by trees—almost every window framed a leafy view. We used to joke, “C&A, sitting in a tree,” and that tree became a quiet symbol of growth, connection, and building something meaningful together.

With a Brady Bunch of nine kids between us, we’re a true modern family with deep roots and wide branches—just like a strong family tree. HayTree represents who we are: grounded, growing, and here to help others build their corner of the web with care.

Curt & Angie Hayman
